Welcome to the HealthFit Club

Your very own ultimate assister to healthy lifestyle and better life. Here, you will get all types of health routines you want considering you go to gym or not, etc., joining our club will not only help you feel happy and be healthy and fit but also energised and motivated to do your work.

Disclaimer- only for students from 6th to 8th

If you go to gym

  • Refreshing till 6:10 after waking at 6.
  • Bathing till 6:25.
  • Breakfast from 6:30 - 7:00 preferrably Moong Dal Chila or a cup of milk with banana or bread and butter.
  • 7:00 to 7:45 in school bus and from 8 to 2 in the school.
  • Till 2:45 in the school bus and then in home.
  • Having a power nap afterwards is best till 3:15 something after changing clothes.
  • After having lunch preferrably a bit light till 3:30 complete homework till 5 in the evening.
  • Going to gym at this time is the best or if there is tuition or something you can afterwards.
  • Go to gym and start light in earlier days and after having confidence start doing the heavy exercise.
  • After the gym till 6-7 something prefer having milk and something light like a banana or any other fruit.
  • Till 7:30 do anything you like and then have dinner preferrably something with protein and full of fibers like green veggies obviously conataining the components of food also at max till 8.
  • Have a 'me' time and write a diary about what all happened that day and do something you like for example painting and preferrably not playing games in electronic devices till around 9.
  • If work is not completed after 9:00 do it and prefer having a good night sleep around 10, before is obviously better but not too soon.

If you don't go to gym

  • Refreshing till 6:10 after waking at 6.
  • Bathing till 6:25.
  • Breakfast from 6:30 - 7:00 preferrably Poha or a cup of milk with cornflakes.
  • 7:00 to 7:45 in school bus and from 8 to 2 in the school.
  • Till 2:45 in the school bus and then in home.
  • Having a power nap afterwards is best till 3:15 something after changing clothes.
  • After having lunch preferrably a bit light till 3:30 complete homework till 5 in the evening.
  • Doing some yoga or exercise at this time (prefer doing alternate days - one day yoga, another exercise)is the best or if there is tuition or something you can afterwards.
  • Go to gym and start light in earlier days and after having confidence start doing the heavy exercise.
  • After the gym till 6-7 something prefer having milk and something light like a banana or any other fruit.
  • Till 7:30 do anything you like and then have dinner preferrably something with vitamins-minerals and full of fibers like green veggies obviously conataining the components of food also have food till at max 8.
  • Have a 'me' time and write a diary about what all happened throughout the day and do something you like for example painting and preferrably not playing games in electronic devices till around 9.
  • If work is not completed after 9:00 do it and prefer having a good night sleep around 10, before is obviously better but not too soon.
Caution - Both plans have difference between them so just don't think they are the same.
